About Educrave

Educrave serves as a platform, & promotes the voluntary contribution of the community education, involving teaching & mentoring various students in their academics.
It paves path for helping our society and contributing for the overall upliftment of the students specially from the underprivileged and poor backgrounds who can't afford to enroll themselves into coaching classes and other courses.
